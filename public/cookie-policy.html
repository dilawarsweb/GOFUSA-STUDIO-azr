Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow websites to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. This helps us provide you with a better, faster, and safer experience.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of our website.

Gofusa Studio uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They also help limit the number of times you see an advertisement and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They allow our website to link your actions during a browser session.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us recognize you as a returning visitor and remember your preferences.

First-Party Cookies

These cookies are set directly by Gofusa Studio. They allow us to provide core website functionality and understand how you use our website.

Third-Party Cookies

These cookies are set by third parties we work with, such as Google Analytics and advertising partners. They help us analyze website traffic and deliver targeted advertisements.

We use several third-party services that may set cookies on your device when you visit our website:

Google Services

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our website
  • Google AdSense: Serves personalized advertisements
  • Google Fonts: Provides web fonts for our website design

Social Media Platforms

When you interact with social media buttons or widgets on our website (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.), these platforms may set cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Analytics and Tracking

We use various analytics tools to collect information about how visitors use our website. This information helps us improve our website and services. These tools use cookies to collect data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Links clicked and buttons pressed
  • Browser and device information
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referring website or source

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising

You can opt out of targeted advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Network Advertising Initiative

Do Not Track

Some browsers have a Do Not Track feature that lets you tell websites you do not want your online activities tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to Do Not Track signals, so our website does not respond to these signals at this time.

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as:

  • Number of visitors to our website
  • Where visitors came from before reaching our website
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Time spent on our website
  • Browser and device information

Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. This add-on prevents the Google Analytics JavaScript from sharing information with Google Analytics about your visit.

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months from the date of collection. After this period, the data is automatically deleted.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. These cookies help:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see an advertisement
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Understand whether you clicked on an advertisement

Personalized Advertising

Google and its partners use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history and interests. You can control personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Adjusting your ad preferences
  • Opting out of personalized advertising completely

Third-Party Ad Networks

We may work with other advertising networks that use cookies to serve targeted advertisements. These networks have their own privacy policies and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.
