Advertising Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Advertising Policy explains how Gofusa Studio uses advertising on our website, including information about third-party advertising services, data collection, and your choices regarding personalized advertising.

We use advertising as one way to support our website and provide free content to our visitors. We are committed to maintaining transparency about our advertising practices and complying with all applicable laws and regulations.

By using our website, you consent to the advertising practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.

Overview

We use Google AdSense, a service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

How Google AdSense Works

Google AdSense analyzes the content of our pages and your browsing behavior to display relevant advertisements. This process involves:

  • Analyzing the content of the page you are viewing
  • Reviewing your browsing history across the Google advertising network
  • Using cookies to track your interests and preferences
  • Delivering ads that match your interests and the page content

Google Publisher Requirements

As a Google AdSense publisher, we comply with all Google Publisher Policies and Restrictions, including:

  • Maintaining original, high-quality content
  • Ensuring a positive user experience
  • Complying with copyright and intellectual property laws
  • Not placing ads on prohibited content
  • Not engaging in invalid click activity

Display Ads

Display ads are graphical advertisements that appear in various locations on our website. These may include banner ads, sidebar ads, and in-content ads. Display ads can be static images or animated graphics.

Text Ads

Text ads are simple, text-based advertisements that blend with our website content. These ads typically consist of a headline, description, and URL.

Responsive Ads

Responsive ads automatically adjust their size, appearance, and format to fit available ad spaces on our website. These ads provide a seamless experience across different devices and screen sizes.

Native Ads

Native ads are designed to match the look, feel, and function of our website content. These ads provide a less intrusive advertising experience while clearly being labeled as advertisements.

In addition to Google AdSense, we may work with other third-party advertising vendors and networks. These vendors may also use cookies and similar technologies to collect information and serve advertisements.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party advertising vendors use cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your past visits to our website and other websites
  • Track the performance of advertising campaigns
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ad too many times
  • Provide reports on ad impressions, clicks, and interactions

Vendor Compliance

All third-party advertising vendors we work with are required to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

Data Collection

Through our advertising services, the following types of data may be collected:

  • IP address and general location information
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • Ad interactions and clicks

Data Usage

This data is used to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Improve ad targeting and relevance
  • Prevent fraud and invalid ad activity
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. However, advertising partners may collect data directly through their own cookies and tracking technologies as described in our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.

Google Ads Settings

You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google by visiting Google Ads Settings. From there, you can:

  • Turn off personalized ads
  • Review and edit your ad preferences
  • See why you saw specific ads
  • Block specific advertisers

Industry Opt-Out Tools

You can also opt out of targeted advertising from participating companies by visiting:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

Browser Settings

You can also manage advertising cookies through your browser settings. However, note that blocking all cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

Mobile Device Settings

On mobile devices, you can limit ad tracking through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

We maintain strict standards regarding the content on our website and the advertisements we display. We prohibit advertisements that:

  • Promote illegal products, services, or activities
  • Contain sexually explicit or adult content
  • Promote violence, hatred, or discrimination
  • Make false or misleading claims
  • Violate intellectual property rights
  • Contain malware, viruses, or malicious code
  • Engage in phishing or identity theft
  • Promote dangerous products or services

If you see an advertisement that violates these standards, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.
